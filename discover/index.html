Discover Greenwire

Greenpeace Greenwire USA is an online community that offers support to anyone in the USA who wants to make an active contribution to the goals Greenpeace seeks in its campaigns.

It is an online platform for people who want to join activities of Greenpeace, or for people with similar goals who want to organize their own activities.

On Greenpeace Greenwire USA you can search for support, share information and have fun connecting with others who share your goals.
